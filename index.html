About Us

Araise eSports EU is a professional and ambitious team based in Germany, established on 05.09.2023. We are represented in Fortnite, Valorant, Rocket League and Overwatch. Our biggest priority is to achieve our and goals and realise everything we can imagine. Cohesion and common progress as well as the ability to work together as a team, are very important to us. At Araise we understand that hard work isn‘t just a motto; it‘s our path to victory! Join us as we rise together! #AraiseOnTop
